Frequently Asked Questions

What is YojnaConnect, and how does it work?
YojnaConnect is an AI-powered platform that simplifies access to government schemes and services. It helps users discover schemes they are eligible for, provides personalized guidance, verifies necessary documents, and streamlines the application process. By creating a profile, you can receive customized recommendations tailored to your needs, making government benefits more accessible than ever.
Is YojnaConnect free to use?
Yes, YojnaConnect is completely free to use! Our mission is to make government schemes and services easily accessible to everyone. Simply sign up, fill in your details, and start exploring the opportunities available to you.
How does YojnaConnect ensure the security of my data?
We prioritize your privacy and data security. YojnaConnect uses robust encryption and secure servers to protect your information. We adhere to strict privacy policies and only use your data to match you with relevant schemes. Your personal information will never be shared without your consent.
Can YojnaConnect help me track my application status?
Absolutely! YojnaConnect offers an application tracking feature that allows you to monitor the status of your submitted applications in real time. You’ll also receive notifications and updates to ensure you don’t miss any critical deadlines or communications.
